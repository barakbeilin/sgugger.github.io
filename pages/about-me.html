About me

I'm a Math and a Computer Science teacher passionate about data science, deep learning in particular.

I've taught for seven years in France in CPGE, which is a two-year intensive full-time course preparing top high school graduates for the entrance examination of French engineering and business schools. Each year I had a group of 40-48 students and we covered general undergrad Mathematics (calculus, real analysis, linear algebra) and introduction to programming (in Python). I then moved to New York City and wrote several textbooks covering this course, all published at Dunod editions.

I discovered deep learning through the excellent MOOK of fast.ai taught by Jeremy Howard, and have become pretty passionate about it.

This blog is a mean for me to explain the things I learn as I delve further into machine learning in general, because I think that's the best way one can completely appropriate himself knowledge.
